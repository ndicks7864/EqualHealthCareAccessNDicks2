Why did I choose the topic of equal healthcare access?

Although I have no personal struggle in accessing sufficient healthcare, the topic of equal healthcare access for all interests me because I believe the sufficiency of healthcare access an individual has should not be determined on that individualsâ€™ financial status. Besides my stance behind the right to equal healthcare access, I also choose this topic because the effects of unequal access to healthcare across the nation causes problems such as financial burdens (debt), which can result in any of the following: poor hygiene, miscare of self, higher mortality and disability rates, and much more.
